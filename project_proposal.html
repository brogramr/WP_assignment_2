Project Proposal

Web Based School Management System

Description

  • A Web-based School Management System, that serves the purpose for providing the students and teachers, a full-fledge and responsive management platform that caters major management and record-handling issues for teachers and academic and course-related issues for the students; both in a well-organized and functional manner.We will provide a web based school management system for school of any scale where students can view their attendance and marks, where teacher's don't have to keep records of marks, grades and attendance on multiple sheets, and where administration is not worried about keeping record of the students, Faculty and the fee management issue .
